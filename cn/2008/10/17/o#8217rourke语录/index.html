O&#8217;Rourke语录

2008-10-17
 
今天是国际反贫困日(International
Day for the Eradication of Poverty)。今年的主题是Human Rights and Dignity of
People Living in Poverty，大致说贫困也是对人权的侵犯，改善人权有助于消除贫困。巧的是，公司的Quote
of the day便是P.J. O’Rourke的一句：
 
       
    You can’t get rid of poverty by giving people
money.
 
消除贫困，眼下只谈风月。P.J.
O’Rourke是现在美国的一个政治讽刺作家。这个行当，讲究风趣机智(wit)，钱钟书那种风格便是。随手附上些O’Rourke的语录。


Marriage（婚姻男女）
 
1. Staying married may have long-term benefits. You can elicit much more
sympathy from friends over a bad marriage than you ever can from a good
divorce.
 
2. The proper behavior all through the holiday season is to be drunk. This
drunkenness culminates on New Year’s Eve, when you get so drunk you kiss the
person you’re married to.
 
3. There are a number of mechanical devices which increase sexual arousal,
particularly in women. Chief among these is the Mercedes-Benz 380SL
convertible.
 
4. There is one thing women can never take away from men. We die
sooner.
 
Adolescent and Education（青少年教育）
 
1. Because of their size, parents may be difficult to discipline
properly.
 
2. Even very young children need to be informed about dying. Explain the
concept of death very carefully to your child. This will make threatening him
with it much more effective.
 
3. Everybody knows how to raise children, except the people who have
them.
 
4. If you are young and you drink a great deal it will spoil your health,
slow your mind, make you fat – in other words, turn you into an adult.
 
5. Let’s reintroduce corporal punishment in the schools – and use it on the
teachers.
 
6. The weirder you’re going to behave, the more normal you should look. It
works in reverse, too. When I see a kid with three or four rings in his nose, I
know there is absolutely nothing extraordinary about that person.
 
7. You know your children are growing up when they stop asking you where
they came from and refuse to tell you where they’re going.
 
Politics（政治）
 
1. Every government is a parliament of whores. The trouble is, in a
democracy, the whores are us.
 
2. Feeling good about government is like looking on the bright side of any
catastrophe. When you quit looking on the bright side, the catastrophe is still
there.
 
3. Giving money and power to government is like giving whiskey and car keys
to teenage boys.
 
4. If government were a product, selling it would be illegal.
 
5. The Democrats are the party that says government will make you smarter,
taller, richer, and remove the crabgrass on your lawn. The Republicans are the
party that says government doesn’t work and then they get elected and prove
it.
 
6. The mystery of government is not how Washington works but how to make it
stop.
 
7. There is only one basic human right, the right to do as you damn well
please. And with it comes the only basic human duty, the duty to take the
consequences.
 
8. Whatever it is that the government does, sensible Americans would prefer
that the government does it to somebody else. This is the idea behind foreign
policy.
 
9. When buying and selling are controlled by legislation, the first things
to be bought and sold are legislators.
 
Miscellaneous（其他）
 
1. After all, what is your host’s purpose in having a party? Surely not for
you to enjoy yourself; if that were their sole purpose, they’d have simply sent
champagne and women over to your place by taxi.
 
2. Always read something that will make you look good if you die in the
middle of it.
 
3. Gossip is what you say about the objects of flattery when they aren’t
present.
 
4. Never wear anything that panics the cat.
 
5. There’s one more terrifying fact about old people: I’m going to be one
soon.
 
6. It is better to spend money like there’s no tomorrow, than to spend
tonight like there’s no money.
