2013/08/25

You will get rich information for your SAS session(definitely more than you need) by submitting:

%put all; *all system macro variables;

proc setinit; *all licensed software;
run;

proc product_status;

run;

proc options; *options;
run;

proc javainfo;  *Java infor;
run;

proc registry list; *registry;
run;
